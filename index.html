Biography

I am currently a PhD student in School of Mathematics and Statistics, The University of Melbourne, supervised by Dr.Mingming Gong and Prof.Howard Bondell. I obtained my M.S. degree of Signal and Information Processing in 2020, supervised by Prof.Zhibin Pan in Faculty of Electronic and Information Engineering at Xi’an Jiaotong University, Xi’an, P.R. China. Prior that, I received the B.S. degree in Information Engineering from the same university, in 2017. During 09/2018-04/2019, I enjoyed half a year’s great time in Arts et Métiers, Angers, France.

My recent research interests are on Causal Discovery, Deep Generative Models and Domain Adaptation.
